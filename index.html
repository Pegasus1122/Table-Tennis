Inspiration




My biggest table tennis inspiration is probably my dad. He used to be really good at table tennis when he was in college. He was the one who taught me how to play and practices with me daily. He encourages me to try my best and keep playing. My dad always has words of improvement after every game. After that, I try to improve and try not to make the same mistake. After playing table tennis with my dad for a few months, I think I have improved as a table tennis player.
